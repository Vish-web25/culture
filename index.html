Diwali – Festival of Lights (India)

Diwali is a celebration of light over darkness. Families decorate homes with diyas 🪔, burst firecrackers 🎆, and share sweets. It’s one of the most joyful and colorful festivals in India.

🎭💃

Rio Carnival (Brazil)

The Rio Carnival is a world-famous party filled with parades, samba dancers 💃, and vibrant costumes 🎭. Streets come alive with energy, music, and non-stop celebration.

🧧🐉

Chinese New Year (China)

This festival marks the start of the lunar year. People give red envelopes 🧧, watch dragon dances 🐉, and enjoy fireworks. It is a time of family reunion and good fortune.
